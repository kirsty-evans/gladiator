# Gladiator

A pain management app for people with chronic pain.

## Current Status
Early development
        
## Planned Features:
- Log pain levels throughout the day
- Analyse pain history to identify patterns and trends along with health data like step count, heart rate, sleep quality, etc.
- Provide insights and recommendations based on pain history
- Develop dynamic exercise plans based on current pain level and pain history
- Develop a pacing plan - balancing activity and rest to avoid flare-ups
- Predict flare-ups and manage accordingly
- Form good habits to reduce pain
- Smart watch integration
